Introduction

Welcome to Near Around Me. This Privacy Policy explains what information our iOS app collects and how we use it.

By using Near Around Me, you agree to this privacy policy.

Information We Collect

Location Information

What We Collect:

  • Your device's approximate location (coarse location)
  • Collected only when you actively use the app

How We Use It:

  • To show you nearby places on the map
  • To calculate distances to places
  • To provide search results for your area

Storage:

  • Not stored anywhere - used in real-time only
  • Automatically discarded when you close the app

Search History

What We Collect:

How We Store It:

  • Never sent to any servers
  • You can clear it anytime in the app

How We Use Your Information

We use your information only for:
